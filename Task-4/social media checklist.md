  Start editing…✅ Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use


✔️ Think before you post anything online


✔️ Use respectful and polite language


✔️ Protect your personal information (phone number, address, etc.)


✔️ Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)


✔️ Maintain a professional and positive online image



❌ Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use


❌ Don’t share passwords or sensitive information


❌ Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content


❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments


❌ Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people


❌ Don’t overshare personal life details publicly
